Goals/Challenges

Some goals and challenges I have that I am aiming to achieve and overcome are creating a slideshow for my photographs, or dividing them equally in concise columns/rows. Also, I want to edit the aspect ratios of the images and make sure they fit together well on a webpage without compromising the quality of the images, or making them too small to capture the details within. Similar to Research Site A & C I would like to make each image a clickable link that leads to either an individual landing page, or hover in the center of the screen to highlight each image in some way, as well as some text to describe it. I am eager to adopt some of the design decisions made in the research sites I chose, but also am aware of the challenge it proposes. Many of those elements are javascript or jquery heavy and may require more practice of how those languages work, from my end. Yet, I don’t want to reach too far and end up disappointed with mediocre results. Instead it’s a long-term goal that will keep me engaged to dive deeper into the front-end development world and continue sharpening my skills so that I can design a the ideal website I picture in my head. Other goals and challenges are to decide on a consistent design aesthetic for my final website in terms of chosen fonts and colors. I am discerning between a minimalist white/black palette to add contrast against the content or an eye popping vibrant neon colors kind of palette to hold consistent with the content. I have generated a style tile to help me visualize how the color palette would actually look on screen, to help me make a final decision.
